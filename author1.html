About Josh Thompsons

Josh Thompsons is an avid learner when it comes to technology and computers. Any new innovations or inventions, Josh knows about it. He is passionate about learning and knowing any basic or advanced technical skills within the industry.

As a child, he was always fascinated with how things worked. Whether it was taking something apart, or trying to fix his broken computer, Josh was always messing around with something. Josh is also highly driven by data and scientific facts that help him form new conclusions about what's possible with new technology.

He is a self-taught programmer and so-called "data freak." His skills range from building computers to writing HTML code for blogs to now being an author on Amazon. When it comes to the tech industry, there isn't much Josh won't take a look at.

New projects are his passion, so he is pretty much working on some sort of project in his free time.
